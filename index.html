Five Interesting Facts

Did you know any of these?

  • Glaciers and ice sheets hold about 69% of the world's fresh water. .That's over half!
  • The fastest gust of wind ever recorded here on Earch was a whopping 253 miles per hour.
  • Mount Everest is bigger now than the last time it was measured. Due to plate tectonics it now stands 29,031 feet tall!.
  • The Canary Islands are actually named after a latin phrase for dogs, not canary birds like many assume.
  • All giant pandas in zoos across the world are on loan from China!
